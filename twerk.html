Origin

Emerging in New Orleans with the birth of the 'bounce' culture is intimately related to the empowerment of the LGBT community. This type of dance has its origin in African dances, such as the 'baikoko mapouka', and exists all over the world, diffused by the African diaspora: Asia, South America or Central America. In other cultures, these dances are not gender-biased and are danced indifferently by both men and women, and even by boys and girls. It has been by westernization that they have gone through the process of sexualization and have acquired a feminine tinge.
