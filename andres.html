ANDRÉS, CARNE DE RES
Cl 3 kr 11 A - 56. Chía, Cundinamarca
star_fill4.6

Andrés Carne de Res, a unique restaurant in the world. Their store is in Chía, half an hour from Bogotá, at the foot of a road whose traffic jams (stretches) lengthen the journey up to two hours. Does not matter. Hundreds of clients come to live the experience as if they were on a pilgrimage to a sanctuary. "Andrés is for dancing, not for eating," they had told me days before. Not true, almost all of their dishes are good despite their kitchens dispatching chill figures. He will have something when he appears in position 24 in the list of The World 50 Best Latin America, which in 2013 headed the restaurant of Gastón Acurio (Astrid and Gaston) in Lima.
