EL GOCE PAGANO
Diagonal 20 a # 0 82, Bogotá, D.C.
star_fill4.5

With more than 30 years of offering one of the tastiest courses in the city, Pagan Enjoyment, it has an identity like no other in terms of musical selection. Its owner is the one in charge of putting on the music and animating the rumba nights. Don't forget to order a delicious Cuban Mojito.

This is the enjoyment that was from 1978 to 1994 and then it was transferred to the Waters or Germania in front of the U de los nades, environmental axis. This is an old house from the year 1745 suitable in a single space, three bars and a large fireplace.
