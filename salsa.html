Origin

Reggaetón is a musical genre that has its origins in the 90s. It was born as an underground phenomenon between the rural neighborhoods of Panama and Puerto Rico, a variant of reggae and Hip-Hop in Spanish, influenced by Latin rhythms and taking as a rhythmic basis the reggae danceHall better known as dembow originally from Jamaica. Reggaetón makes its first steps as a musical genre after many Panamanian artists began to experiment, covering famous dancehall reggae hits in Spanish, which became known as "Reggae in Spanish".
