ARMANDO RECORDS
Avenida Calle 85#14-46, Bogotá, D.C.
star_fill4.1

On the roof of an old building on 85 is Armando Records. It is said that it is the best bar in Bogotá, the coolest, the most playful, the one that was reviewed in a New York magazine for its originality, according to an urban myth that runs around there.

The place is beautiful, the terrace is wide, it has many fireplaces that keep it warm, perhaps too much when dancing is in full swing. The toilets are clean and have paper, and there is not a lady looking at you suspiciously
