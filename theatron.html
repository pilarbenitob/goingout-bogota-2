THEATRON
Calle 58 Bis No.10 - 32, Bogotá, D.C.
star_fill4.4

Theatron is a complex of gay bars, discos and clubs located in the Chapinero Alto neighborhood. It is the nerve center of the gay community in Bogotá. Theatron is considered one of the largest nightclubs in Latin America and one of the largest in the world. It can be frequented by up to 5000 people on Saturday night.

Built in what was an old theater, the complex has 16 different atmospheres and atmospheres, one of them exclusively for female audiences.3 The sixteenth room (El Muro) was opened to the public on the club's 16th anniversary. The main hall performs a well-known drag show every Saturday, in addition to different concerts and shows.
