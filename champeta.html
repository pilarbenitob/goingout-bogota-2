Champeta

Colombia

Origin

The Champeta is a musical and cultural phenomenon, in fact social and musical genre of local origin in the areas of African descent and not as a genre of the slums of Cartagena de Indias since it is a genre currently known throughout the world, later extending to other cities in the region as Barranquilla and Santa Marta among others; linked to the culture of the corregimiento of Palenque de San Basilio and influenced by genres of African colonies as well as the African continent.
