Celia Cruz

Cuba

About

(Havana; October 21, 1925 - Fort Lee, New Jersey; July 16, 2003) was a Cuban salsa singer who had her career in Cuba and the United States.

Celia was the most famous and highest-paid Latin American singer of the 20th century, with twenty-two gold albums to her name, among many other recognitions including 9 grammy. Known throughout the world for her excellent quality as a singer and for her famous phrase "Sugar!", Symbol of her philosophy before life and of her pride in being Cuban. His style transcended times and remained in force for more than fifty years.
