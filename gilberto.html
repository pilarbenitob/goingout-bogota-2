Gilberto Santa Rosa

Puerto Rico

About

Gilberto Santa Rosa Cortés, (Santurce, Puerto Rico, August 21, 1962), better known as "the gentleman of salsa", is a singer of this tropical genre as well as romantic ballads. He was born the son of a draftsman of engineering drawings and an operator of the first IBM computers on the island. Among other well-known songs are If you do not see me crying, Those tears, I do not call you nor I seek you, To love yourself there is no need to see each other, Live without it, Consciousness, Forgive me, Without Will, You can say, I do not want anything, Shadow Crazy, they told me about her again, Countdown, etc. He even covered the songs Caballo Viejo by Simón Díaz, Dime por qué by Ismael Rivera and Buenas Noches Don David by Ricardo Arjona.
