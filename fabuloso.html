EL FABULOSO
Ac. 85 #14-5, Bogotá, D.C.
star_fill3.8

If you dance salsa or at least try, but also want to hear reggaeton and sing rock songs in Spanish, this is your place. It sounds like everything from merengue to indie music. And if you don't want rumba, go to eat something early.

The site is characterized by its huge windows and rustic materials. Tables, chairs, and bars are made of wood, metal, and cement; the ceiling, of different heights, is decorated with irregularly cut wood, and the bathroom sinks are metal barrels.
